Team: BetterLateThanNever

When starting off this project, none of us really knew what Autism was, or what it meant. After making this web app, we learned many terms and facts about autism and famous people with autism. We hope that people we visit this site can learn more about autism and have fun in the process!
Who - Who would ever use this web app? Autism is a very serious disorder and affects 1 in 68 children! Though Autism is a relatively known disorder, not everyone knows exactly what Autism is and its symptoms! Autism Flip Cards is here to inform any user about some important Autism terms! Parents can use the app to learn about Autism and prepare themselves for a possible child with autism. This web app can also give students a quick way to study for an exam on Autism!
What - What is the Autism Flip Cards web app!? The Autism Flip Cards web app gives index like cards with quick terms relative to Autism on them! The user can learn new key terms and definitions that they never knew before. This is a very simple web app breaks down complicated Autism terms into more simpler and easier to understand definitions. Along with key Autism terms there are also fun facts about famous people with Autism thrown into the mix!
