Installation

Click to Flip

1) Open up an internet browser of your choice.
2) In the address bar, type in http://www.github.com/nattyproton.
3) Click on repositories.
4) Find and click on AutismAppJam2015.
5) Click Download ZIP.
6) Unzip the file.
7) Open the file, click on the folder app, then click on index.
8) Have fun!

General Use

Click to Flip

Upon opening the website, you are shown a list of flashcards that you can study. This doubles as the home page as well. From here and from any other page on the website, you can click on the logo, the flashcard library tab, the about tab, the how to use it tab, and the contact us tab. The logo takes you back to the home page. The flashcard library takes you to a page where the flashcards are shown at a smaller resolution so you can see exactly what is in the flashcard stack. The about page explains our philosophy for why we developed this website. How to use it is a simple tutorial on how to gain access to the website, and has a general use guide. Contact us provides contact information of everyone who contributed to the project. Using the footer's "Go to top" tab, you can return to the very top of the page at any time.
